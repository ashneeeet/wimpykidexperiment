My Family

I have a family that I find tough to survive in. No, not because they are bad, but because I'm too good for them. Nah, not a narcissistic person, but just a big introvert who should be surrounded by better humans (pets are allowed, except pigs). If I'd want to remove someone from my family, it'll be Manny, my little brother, who is cute to the world but a menace to me tbh. I have an overprotectice and overenthusiastic mum, a 9-5 office-slave dad who has to cope with the family drama that my mum keeps creating, a band-enthusiast and useless older brother too.

My Social Life

A middle schooler's social life usually sucks if he's not popular at school and is mostly in some corner trying to hide from bullies. Yeah, I'm talking about my own self. My social life includes my school, my neighbourhood, any party if I luckily get invited to once in a blue moon, and thus, ofcourse, pretty much non-existent.
